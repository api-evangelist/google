Google introduced Gemma 4 12B, a multimodal model featuring an encoder-free architecture that processes vision and audio inputs directly without separate encoders. The model runs locally on consumer devices with 16GB VRAM and includes new macOS desktop applications for offline AI interaction, with support for agentic reasoning, video understanding, and speech recognition through tools like LiteRT-LM.
