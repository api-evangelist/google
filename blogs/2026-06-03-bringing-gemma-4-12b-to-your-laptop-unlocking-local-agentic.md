Google's newest open model, Gemma 4 12B, enables developers to run advanced AI capabilities directly on standard laptops through the Google AI Edge platform. The announcement introduces three key applications: Google AI Edge Gallery for local data analysis and coding, Google AI Edge Eloquent for voice-powered text editing, and LiteRT-LM with a new local serving feature that creates OpenAI-compatible endpoints for building agentic workflows entirely on-device while maintaining data privacy.
